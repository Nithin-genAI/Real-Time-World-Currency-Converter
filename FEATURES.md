# 🚀 Detailed Features & User Guide

Welcome to **Nithin Corporate Currency Converter**. This guide provides a detailed breakdown of all the powerful features designed to make your financial tracking seamless, accurate, and aesthetic.

---

## 1. 💎 Smart Conversion Engine
The heart of the application is a high-performance conversion engine that provides instant results.
- **Real-Time Rates**: Connects directly to **ExchangeRate-API** for the most accurate global currency values.
- **Auto-Detect & Debounce**: No need to click "Convert" every time. The app detects your input and updates the results automatically with a smooth 500ms debounce.
- **Instant Swap**: Effortlessly switch between `BASE` and `TARGET` currencies with the central swap button (or hit `Ctrl + S`).
- **Copy to Clipboard**: With the 📋 button, you can instantly copy the full conversion result and current rate to your clipboard for use in reports or messages.

## 2. 📊 Historical Trend Analysis
Visualize market movements over time with our clean, high-performance charting system.
- **Official ECB Data**: Unlike other tools that use simulated data, our charts pull official historical rates from the **European Central Bank (ECB)** via the Frankfurter API.
- **Multiple Timeframes**: 
  - **7D**: Catch short-term fluctuations.
  - **30D**: Observe monthly stability.
  - **3M**: Perfect for quarterly planning.
  - **1Y**: Deep-dive into annual trends.
- **Dynamic Stats**: The chart automatically calculates **High**, **Low**, and **Average** rates for the selected period to give you instant insight.

## 3. ⚖️ Multi-Currency Comparison
Compare your base currency against a curated list of the world's most stable and popular currencies.
- **Live Comparison Table**: See how your currency stands against EUR, GBP, JPY, CAD, and more at a single glance.
- **Sorting Logic**: Sort your comparison table by "Top 10" (Highest value) or alphabetical order.
- **Quick Favorites**: Add any pair directly from the table to your Favorites Hub with the ☆ button.

## 4. ⭐ Favorites Hub
Personalize your workspace by pinning the currency pairs you care about most.
- **Real-Time Cards**: Each favorite pair is rendered as a clean glassmorphism card that shows its current live rate.
- **One-Click Convert**: Click any favorite card to instantly load that pair into the main converter for detailed math.
- **Persistant Storage**: Your favorites are saved to your browser's local storage, meaning they'll be there every time you return.

## 5. 🧮 Integrated Smart Calculator
Never leave the app to do math.
- **Modal Access**: Click the ➕ button next to the amount input to open a sleek, glass-styled calculator.
- **Apply to Converter**: Once you calculate a complex value (like an invoice total), click the `=` button to instantly apply that result to your conversion.

## 6. 🌙 Moonlight Midnight Theme
Experience the most aesthetic "Dark Mode" in the industry.
- **Moonlight Aesthetics**: A deep midnight blue theme designed with radial mesh gradients and soft indigo glows.
- **Eye Optimization**: Every color has been carefully selected to reduce blue light strain while maintaining high contrast for market data.
- **Glassmorphism**: Premium frosted-glass effects that give the interface a sense of layers and modern depth.

## 7. ⌨️ Pro Productivity Shortcuts
For power users who want to move as fast as the markets.
- **`Ctrl + S`**: Swap your From/To currencies.
- **`Enter`**: Refresh the conversion result.
- **`Ctrl + C`**: Copy the conversion result (when the result section is visible).
- **`Esc`**: Close any open modals or dropdowns.

## 8. 📶 Offline Resilience
Built with local caching layers to keep you informed even when the signal is weak.
- **Offline Banner**: Automatically detects your connection status and notifies you if you are viewing cached data.
- **Intelligent Caching**: Rates and historical data are stored locally for several hours to ensure the app stays fast and functional.
